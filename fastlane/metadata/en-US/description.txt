Timai is the mobile time tracking app for Kimai.

FEATURES:
• Easy time tracking with Kimai integration
• Offline mode: Work without internet connection
• Automatic synchronization when connection is restored
• Detailed reports and analytics
• Cache management for optimal performance
• Multi-language: German and English

REQUIREMENTS:
• Kimai installation (version 0.5.0 or higher)
• API token from your Kimai server

KIMAI:
Kimai is a free open-source time tracking tool for teams and freelancers.

Support: Visit our website for help and documentation.
